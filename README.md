CUSTOMIZABLE — ADJUST WORK AND BREAK DURATIONS TO FIT YOUR STYLE
AUTO IDLE — AUTOMATICALLY PAUSES WHEN YOU STEP AWAY
SILENT MODE — MUTE NOTIFICATIONS FOR SHARED SPACES
STARTUP OPTION — LAUNCH AUTOMATICALLY WHEN YOU LOG IN
